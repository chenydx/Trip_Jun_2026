### Budget Lodging Logic

This itinerary uses cheaper lodging outside the most expensive park gateway towns.

- Instead of Jackson: stay in **Victor / Driggs**
- Instead of West Yellowstone: stay in **Island Park**
- Keep **Gardiner** for one night because it is important for Mammoth Hot Springs and Lamar Valley
- End in **Idaho Falls** to reduce the final return drive to Salt Lake City

### Key Trade-Off

This route saves money, but adds daily driving time:

- Victor / Driggs adds Teton Pass driving
- Island Park adds extra driving through Yellowstone’s West Entrance
- Gardiner is still recommended because Livingston would make 6/18 and 6/19 too tiring
- 6/19 is the hardest driving day of the trip

## Day 1 — 6/13: Arrive in Salt Lake City

### Route

Salt Lake City airport / city → local errands

### Drive

0 hr, mostly local city activity

### Lodging

Salt Lake City

### Core Plan

1. Pick up rental car.
2. Check license, fuel, vehicle condition, tire condition, and insurance details.
3. Buy bear spray, water, snacks, breakfast supplies, and simple road-trip food.
4. Have lunch or early dinner.
5. Rest early because the next day starts the long-drive rhythm.

- Meal: 1–1.5 hr
- Local driving: about 0.5 hr

Prioritize supplies and rest. Do not treat this as a sightseeing day.

## Key Warnings and Decision Rules

### 6/14 Taggart Lake

Taggart Lake is optional. If access, parking, or construction is difficult, skip it and focus on:
- Schwabacher Landing
- Mormon Row
- Snake River Overlook

### 6/15 Cascade Canyon

Do not turn Cascade Canyon into a full long hike. The recommended plan is:
- Boat across Jenny Lake
- Hidden Falls
- Inspiration Point
- Short out-and-back into Cascade Canyon
- Return

### 6/17 Hayden Valley

Hayden Valley is best near evening, but do not stay too late because the return to Island Park is long and can be tiring.

### 6/18 Lamar Valley

Gardiner is the right lodging choice for this day. Do not replace Gardiner with Livingston unless the group accepts a much longer night and next-morning drive.

### 6/19 Yellowstone Lake / West Thumb

This is not a hiking day. Keep it simple:
- Yellowstone Lake
- West Thumb
- South Entrance
- Idaho Falls

## Final Recommendation

This budget-conscious itinerary is feasible and keeps the major Grand Teton and Yellowstone highlights. The main compromises are extra driving, less flexibility for sunrise/sunset photography, and reduced hiking depth.

Best overall lodging compromise:

**Salt Lake City 1 night → Victor / Driggs 2 nights → Island Park 2 nights → Gardiner 1 night → Idaho Falls 1 night → Salt Lake City**

Do not replace the Gardiner night with Livingston unless cost is the absolute priority.
