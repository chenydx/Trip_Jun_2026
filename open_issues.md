## 1. Jackson vs Victor / Driggs

### English

**Question:** Should the group stay in Jackson or Victor / Driggs for 6/14 and 6/15?

#### Option A — Jackson

Pros:
- Closer to Grand Teton core sights
- Easier early morning and evening photography
- Less driving after hiking
- More convenient for Jenny Lake, Mormon Row, Oxbow Bend, and Schwabacher Landing

Cons:
- Usually much more expensive
- Parking and traffic can be annoying
- Availability may be limited

Best if:
- Budget allows
- Group wants less driving
- Group wants better sunrise/sunset flexibility

#### Option B — Victor / Driggs

Pros:
- Cheaper than Jackson
- Better budget option
- Still workable for this itinerary

Cons:
- Requires Teton Pass driving
- Adds 45–75 minutes each way depending on destination and traffic
- More tiring after Cascade Canyon / Jenny Lake day
- Less flexible for sunrise and sunset photography

Current recommendation: use **Victor / Driggs** if budget is the main concern. Use **Jackson** if the price difference is acceptable.

### 中文

**问题：** 6/14 和 6/15 住 Jackson，还是住 Victor / Driggs？

#### 选项 A — Jackson

优点：
- 离大提顿核心景点更近
- 早晚拍照更方便
- 徒步后回酒店更轻松
- 去 Jenny Lake、Mormon Row、Oxbow Bend、Schwabacher Landing 都更顺

缺点：
- 通常贵很多
- 停车和交通可能麻烦
- 房源可能紧张

适合：
- 预算允许
- 想少开车
- 想保留日出/日落灵活性

#### 选项 B — Victor / Driggs

优点：
- 比 Jackson 便宜
- 适合预算版
- 这个行程仍然能执行

缺点：
- 需要翻 Teton Pass
- 单程多 45–75 分钟左右，视目的地和路况
- Jenny Lake / Cascade Canyon 徒步后回去更累
- 日出日落机动性下降

当前建议：预算优先住 **Victor / Driggs**；如果 Jackson 价格差可以接受，住 **Jackson** 更舒服。

## 2. Yellowstone In-Park Lodging vs Island Park / West Yellowstone

### English

**Question:** Should the group stay inside Yellowstone for 6/16 or 6/17, or keep Island Park?

Current base plan:
- 6/16: Island Park
- 6/17: Island Park
- 6/18: Gardiner

#### Option A — Keep Island Park

Pros:
- Usually cheaper than in-park lodging
- More availability
- Works for West Entrance access
- Good for budget control

Cons:
- Extra drive from the West Entrance
- Long return after Hayden Valley on 6/17
- Less flexible for early/late park time

#### Option B — Stay Inside Yellowstone

Most useful nights:
1. **6/17 Canyon Lodge** — best single-night upgrade if available. Reduces the long return after Canyon / Hayden Valley.
2. **6/16 Old Faithful area** — helpful for Old Faithful, Upper Geyser Basin, and Grand Prismatic area.
3. **6/18 Mammoth** — nice, but less necessary because Gardiner already works well.

Current recommendation:
- If only one Yellowstone in-park night is affordable: **6/17 Canyon Lodge**
- If two nights are affordable: **6/16 Old Faithful area + 6/17 Canyon Lodge**
- Keep **Gardiner** for 6/18 unless Mammoth is close in price.

### 中文

**问题：** 6/16 或 6/17 要不要住黄石园内？还是继续住 Island Park？

当前基础方案：
- 6/16：Island Park
- 6/17：Island Park
- 6/18：Gardiner

#### 选项 A — 保持 Island Park

优点：
- 通常比园内便宜
- 房源相对多
- 适合从 West Entrance 进出
- 控制预算

缺点：
- 从 West Entrance 到住宿还要额外开
- 6/17 Hayden Valley 后回 Island Park 较累
- 早晚游览灵活性差一些

#### 选项 B — 住黄石园内

最值得考虑的晚上：
1. **6/17 Canyon Lodge** — 最值得升级的一晚，可以减少 Canyon / Hayden Valley 后回 Island Park 的长夜路。
2. **6/16 Old Faithful 区域** — 对 Old Faithful、Upper Geyser Basin、大棱镜区域更方便。
3. **6/18 Mammoth** — 可以，但不是必须，因为 Gardiner 已经比较顺。

当前建议：
- 只升级一晚：优先 **6/17 Canyon Lodge**
- 升级两晚：**6/16 Old Faithful 区域 + 6/17 Canyon Lodge**
- 6/18 继续住 **Gardiner**，除非 Mammoth 和 Gardiner 价格差不多。
