## Why This Update Matters / 为什么这样改

### English

The original 6/19 plan prioritized making 6/20 easier. That worked logistically, but it made 6/19 a rushed exit day.

If the group truly has all day on Saturday 6/20 to return to Salt Lake City, then the better trade-off is:

- spend more time in Yellowstone on 6/19;
- exit through the South Entrance;
- sleep around Jackson / Victor / Driggs;
- drive back to SLC on 6/20.

This shifts the trip from a return-drive optimization to a park-time optimization.
